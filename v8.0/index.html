FaQ

~WHAT IS DATATHON?

Datathon is a datathon competition that brings together the brightest minds in the data science world in Sri Lanka to showcase their data analytics capabilities by developing thought-provoking business solutions.

~HOW CAN I FORM A TEAM DURING REGISTRATION?

During registration, we only accept individual application. Once you are shortlisted, you will be given a list of all the shortlisted participants to form your own team of 5 members from that list.

~CAN I FORM A TEAM OF MEMBERS AND JOIN DATATHON?

Datathon is a datathon competition that brings together the brightest minds in the data science world in Sri Lanka to showcase their data analytics capabilities by developing thought-provoking business solutions.

~IS THERE ANY AGE LIMIT?

If all your friends are shortlisted for this competition after passing the online assessment, you can form your own team. Else, we will help you to form a team.
